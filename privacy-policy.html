WordBubbles: Learn & Play - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2025

Introduction

WordBubbles: Learn & Play ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This privacy policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use our service.

Information We Do Not Collect

WordBubbles is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users, including:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Audio recordings or speech data
  • Any other personal information

How the App Works

WordBubbles operates primarily offline on your device. The app:

  • Uses your device's text-to-speech functionality to pronounce words
  • Loads random background images from Picsum Photos (picsum.photos) to create a visually engaging experience
  • Stores game progress locally on your device only

Third-Party Services

Picsum Photos: Our app displays random background images from Picsum Photos, a service that provides royalty-free images. When the app loads these images, your device makes a request to picsum.photos. We have reviewed these images to ensure they are appropriate for children, but we do not control this third-party service.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

WordBubbles is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We:

  • Do not collect personal information from children under 13
  • Do not require registration or account creation
  • Do not include social features or communication capabilities
  • Do not include advertising or in-app purchases

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data to secure on our servers. All app functionality occurs locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
